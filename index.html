The Avalanche Feature — How Cascading Wins Work

The most celebrated mechanic in Gonzo's Quest is the Avalanche feature — a revolutionary cascading wins system that fundamentally changed how online slots are played. Instead of traditional spinning reels, carved Mayan stone block symbols crash down from above onto a 5×3 grid. When a winning combination lands, the winning symbols explode and disappear, allowing new symbols to fall from above and fill the empty spaces. This chain reaction continues as long as new winning combinations keep forming, giving players the chance to score multiple wins from a single paid spin. The Avalanche feature was groundbreaking when the game launched and has since been adopted and adapted by dozens of other slot developers — proof of its genius design.

Alongside the cascading wins, the Avalanche mechanic comes with a progressive multiplier meter displayed in the top-right corner of the screen. Each consecutive cascade win increases the multiplier level:

  • 1st Avalanche: 1x multiplier — your standard win value.
  • 2nd Avalanche: 2x multiplier — winnings doubled from the second consecutive cascade.
  • 3rd Avalanche: 3x multiplier — three-fold increase on cascading wins.
  • 4th Avalanche: 5x multiplier — the maximum base game multiplier unlocks massive pay potential.

Reaching the 5x multiplier in the base game is where some of the most exciting payouts occur. Players have reported base game wins exceeding 10x their total stake purely through consecutive avalanche chains — without even triggering the Free Falls bonus round.

Free Falls Bonus — What Are Free Spins in Gonzo's Quest?

The primary bonus feature in Gonzo's Quest is called the Free Falls — the slot's version of a free spins round. Since the reels don't actually spin (they cascade), NetEnt appropriately named this bonus feature "Free Falls" to match the game's mechanics. To trigger the Free Falls, players must land three or more Free Fall scatter symbols anywhere on the reels during a paid spin. Landing exactly 3 scatter symbols awards 10 Free Falls, while landing 3+ scatter symbols on two separate betlines simultaneously bumps the award up to 20 Free Falls — a rare and highly rewarding outcome.

What makes the Free Falls truly exceptional is the dramatically enhanced multiplier scale. In the base game, the Avalanche multiplier tops out at 5x. During the Free Falls bonus round, the multiplier scale is boosted significantly:

  • 1st Free Falls Avalanche: 3x multiplier — already triple the base game starting point.

  • 3rd Free Falls Avalanche: 9x multiplier — exceptional payout territory unlocked.
  • 4th Free Falls Avalanche: 15x multiplier — the maximum bonus round multiplier, where legendary wins happen.

The Free Falls round is also re-triggerable — landing 3+ scatter symbols during the bonus round awards additional free falls, extending the bonus and giving players even more chances to hit that 15x multiplier. With a maximum bet of $50 per spin and up to 3,750x the stake as the theoretical max win, a single activated Free Falls round at maximum multiplier and optimal symbol alignment can yield up to $187,500 — truly life-changing slot entertainment.
